;; Won Battles
;------------------------------------------

Trigger battlewinning
    WhenToTest PostBattle

    Condition IsGeneral 
          and WonBattle
          and BattleSuccess >= clear
          and I_ConflictType Normal
		  and BattleOdds >= 0.5
          and BattleOdds < 1.5

    Affects Battlewinner  1  Chance  100
	
;; Lost Battles
;------------------------------------------

Trigger battlelosing
    WhenToTest PostBattle

    Condition IsGeneral 
          and not WonBattle
          and BattleSuccess >= clear
          and I_ConflictType Normal

    Affects Battleloser  1  Chance  100
